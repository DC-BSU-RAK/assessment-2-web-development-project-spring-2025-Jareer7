Our Cabin Classes

Discover a journey tailored to your style, comfort, and budget — whether you're flying Economy, Business, or First Class.

Economy Class

Affordable comfort with generous legroom, in-seat entertainment, and delicious onboard meals.

  • On-demand entertainment
  • Complimentary meals and beverages

Business Class

Luxury redefined with flatbed seats, gourmet dining, and lounge access before you fly.

  • Lie-flat seats with aisle access
  • Fine dining experience
  • Priority check-in and baggage

First Class

The ultimate in privacy and luxury with personal suites, exclusive menus, and world-class service.

  • Private suite with a door
  • Personal butler service
  • Premium amenity kits and bedding
